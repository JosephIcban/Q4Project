CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin (Ibarra)
  • Juan Crisostomo Ibarra is a young, smart, and wealthy man who has a vision for an equal and proper society for his country.
Maria Clara
  • Maria Clara is a beautiful and young woman who is thought to be the daughter of Capitan Tiago.
  • She is the love interest of Ibarra
Father Damaso
  • Father Damaso is a corrupt and unjust Spanish priest who exploits religion as a way to get rich and have power of Filipinos.
  • He is the true father of Maria Clara.
Capitan Tiago
  • Capitan Tiago is a powerful businessman from San Diego and is the "father" of Maria Clara.
Father Salvi
  • Father Salvi is the successor of Father Damaso and the rival of the town's Alferez.
